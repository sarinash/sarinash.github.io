About

I am a Applied Scientist at Panasonic Corporation. I received my Ph.D. and M.Sc. from Tokyo Institute of Technology, where I was advised by Prof. Yoshihiro Miyake, and Prof. Takayuki Nozawa. I recieved my my B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering at Shahid Beheshti University.

My research interests include Human Computer Interaction (HCI), Music, VR/AR, and Time series data analysis.
